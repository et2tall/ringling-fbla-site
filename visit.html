Plan your day at The Ringling

Quick details to help visitors choose a day, understand hours, and plan tickets. For the most accurate info, always verify on the official Ringling website (see Sources).

Hours

Open daily Typical hours

10:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Some Thursdays include extended evening hours for select areas. The museum is closed on major holidays listed on the official site.

Tip Planning

Best visit length

Many visitors spend 2–4 hours to see galleries, the Circus Museum, and gardens comfortably (tours can add more time).

Tickets (simple guidance)

Admission

Buy online or on-site

Ticket options can vary (general admission, tours, and packages). Buying online can help with timed tours and busy days.

Tours

Ca’ d’Zan tours

House tours may require an add-on or separate ticket depending on the day and tour type.

Accessibility

Family tip

Start with the Circus Museum

The Circus Museum is often a high-energy, kid-friendly entry point with big visuals and engaging displays.
